🚀 TrailVerse for ChatGPT

The headline: I launched a TrailVerse app in ChatGPT — it lives in ChatGPT's Apps directory (search Apps for TrailVerse). It's the same park data and planning tools, right inside ChatGPT — plan trips, check live weather and alerts, compare parks, search 470+ NPS sites, and find ranger programs.

Ask things like “plan 5 days in Yellowstone in September” or “compare Zion and Grand Canyon for June” — then keep refining (“add day 4”) in the same chat. Open in ChatGPT

✨ What's new on TrailVerse

On the site itself, I shipped a round of updates — fixes, polish, and features people asked for:

🏞️ Park pages — easier tabs

Same sections as before (overview, alerts, things to do, campgrounds, photos, and more) — I redesigned the tab layout so everything is easier to find in one place.

💬 Trailie — polished

The planner chat you already know, cleaned up a bit. Open Trailie

🎙️ Voice on every page

Tap the mic anywhere on TrailVerse and ask Trailie out loud — no typing, no jumping to a separate screen.

🧭 Explore by Activity

Browse by activity, park type, state, and topic — learn what you can do and find parks that match. Explore by Activity

🚌 Transit tab on park pages

Shuttles, stops, and getting around inside the park — on the park page, in the Transit tab.

🗺️ Map — redesigned

All NPS parks and sites on one map; search a park, see nearby sights and campgrounds, tap a pin for details. Open the map

⚖️ Compare — any park type

Not just the 63 national parks — monuments, recreation areas, historic sites, and more. Compare parks

If a trip went well, I'd love a testimonial or a quick review on a park page — only if you want to. It really helps the next person planning.
